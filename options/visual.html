Why this structure matters
Built around security, custody and clear controls.
The Fund invests in loans secured by first mortgages, with independent supervision from Public Trust and assets held separately by Property Funding Custodians Limited.
The investor case
1st
First mortgage security
Loans are required to be secured by first mortgages over residential, commercial and rural property.
PIE
Managed investment scheme
A registered Managed Investment Scheme structured as a Portfolio Investment Entity in New Zealand.
LVR
Conservative lending settings
Loan to Value Ratios are less than 60%, diversified across property sectors and supported by lending controls.
PT
Independent oversight
Public Trust supervises the Fund and all bank transactions must be verified and authorised by the supervisor.
